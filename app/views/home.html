Is a Biba playground right for your community?

Biba uses the smartphones found in every parents’ pocket or purse to unlock new and exciting games every time a family visits the playground. And it’s not just fun for the kids!

Every gameplay session generates a series of family friendly fitness metrics that let parents track their kids’ progress. We lock this information so that it is 100% private and secure!

After anonymizing and aggregating this data, our system generates specific reports for your community that will provide you with an unparalleled level of understanding and insight!

Privacy is our top priority

We understand that digital privacy is a crucial concern for today’s parents. Biba has been designed with that in mind from day one: all of our games are fully compliant with the Children’s Online Privacy and Protection Act.

What’s more, we ensure that all user data is segmented, anonymized and encrypted so that no information can be traced to a particular phone, family or child.

NO DEVICE DANGER

Parents don’t need to worry about their shiny new smartphone, thanks to Biba’s “refereed” play mode. The device never leaves the caregiver’s hands and lets parents be an active part of the fun.

INCLUSIVE PLAY

Biba games are designed so that every child can easily join in the fun, regardless of any possible limitations on their mobility. That’s the way it should be… because Biba games are for everyone!

GAMES ARE ALWAYS FREE

Mom and Dad don’t need to worry about being nickel-and-dimed for every new game. Biba games are always free and have been certified as fully family friendly by Google Play!
